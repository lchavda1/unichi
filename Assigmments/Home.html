Upcoming Events

Because of the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, the decision has been taken to cancel the Barns Green Half Marathon and 10k races this year

The Barns Green Half Marathon and 10k races will return next year and the races will take place on Sunday 26th September 2021

Why not make it a day out and come and enjoy the Barns Green 1/2M and 10K Races?

There will be entertainment for all the family and ample car parking. Before the races start, why not watch the runnersâ€™ warm-up to a musical routine.

There will be a funfair and ice cream van for the children. In the food area there will be fish and chips, hot dogs and burgers plus teas and cakes. If you wish to dine alfresco then why not bring a picnic and enjoy the atmosphere.

Local outlets include the Queen’s Head pub for fine ales and food, Barns Green Village Store for your Sunday papers and sundries and Sumners Pond Camping and Fishery for refreshments and lunches and the Sports and Social Club on the Village Green where drinks can be purchased.
To top it all off there will be six bands playing on the course for the runners and three bands on the Village Green.

We hope you are able to join us, whether you are a runner or supporter, and enjoy your day at the “races”!
